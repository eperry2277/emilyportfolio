About Our Wolves

  • We have been breeding wolves for more than 15 years, and have never had a pup returned due to undesirable behavior.
  • The breeding wolves we use are named Nymeria and Ghost. Nymeria is a longhaired, dark-grey wolf, about 120 lbs. Ghost is a longhaired, white wolf, about 140 lbs. The majority of their pups end up being somewhere in between their sizes.
  • Before buying a wolf, we reccomend checking state laws regarding wolf ownership and obtain all proper certifications.
  • Before buying a wolf, be aware of the care needed to properly care for a wolf (see below for more information) and that they are non-domesticated animals and we are not liable for aggressive behavior.
